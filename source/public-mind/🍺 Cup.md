
Example things to put in here are:

- Your minimum burn rate that you need to survive
- Your ideal monthly salary to have some breathing room / savings
- Any big expenses you need (like a piece of equipment or a travel expense to go to a conference or event)
- Non monetary things, like specific skills or attention 

If your cup is "overflowing" you can say what kind of things you're looking to support, and what resource you have that is overflowing (could be money, or could be you are getting too much inbound work and want to give it to younger people in the field)
